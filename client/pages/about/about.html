Why is sleep so important?

Sleep is an important component of a person’s physical and mental wellbeing.
33% of adults don’t sleep the recommended 7-8 hours each night

why?
A variety of factors like: higher work demands, stress, and technology

Sleep deprivation is known to affect a person’s daily living. It takes a toll in almost every public indicator such as mortality, accidents and injuries, quality of life, and even simple healthcare utilization

How can music help?


Music therapy has been tried and researched in helping people with a history of sleeping disorders. Simple musical elements like repetition, rhythm, and tune can help the mind relax.

Music could be the solution to helping individuals sleep better at night.
But...
Music is a very subjective and diverse category. This makes it difficult to create a general solution for everyone. That is why we at RestRhythm aim to create a sleeping music environment tailored to you and your needs.

What exactly is RestRhythm?

RestRhythm is a music based website that is aimed at helping sleep deprived young adults customize their musical environment in order to sleep better at night. The addition of soundscapes to music that you know and love will help you find the perfect musical environment for you! If classical music helps you relax and you like the rain, why not the sound of rain on top of your classical music? This is what RestRhythm is designed to do. Our simple design makes it easy for users to quickly add different soundscapes on top of the music of their choice.

Not sure where to start?

No worries. We have recommended playlists ready to go that include genres like lofi, classical music, instrumental pop, and even jazz. If you don’t like any of these options, you can certainly use your own music by using the spotify integration.
